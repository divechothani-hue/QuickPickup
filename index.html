What is QuickPickup App?

QuickPickup is a leading logistics service provider offering a wide range of transportation solutions. With QuickPickup, you can deliver goods reliably. With its vast operations in 22 Indian cities, including cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Pune, Surat, Jaipur and Kolkata, QuickPickup makes deliveries easy.

Looking for on-demand delivery services for business or personal needs? Transport your goods and consignments via a mini-truck, tempo or two-wheeler. Reliable moving of your goods is now just a click away. Download QuickPickup now!

In its 10 years of operation, QuickPickup has transformed logistics, handling everything from small items (e.g., a pin) to large moves (e.g., relocating a penthouse). Key offerings include:

  • On-demand goods transport services via Trucks and Two-Wheelers: From commercial goods to personal goods, our on-demand vehicles offer seamless, cost-effective, and reliable delivery solutions. Choose from mini trucks, tempos, EVs, and two-wheelers to transport goods effortlessly across the city—anytime, anywhere.
  • QuickPickup Enterprise: A complete logistics partner for businesses, offering smart solutions for bulk transportation, distribution, and supply chain management.
  • QuickPickup Packers & Movers: Professional packing and moving services designed for hassle-free relocation of homes
  • QuickPickup Intercity Courier Services: Reliable and timely parcel delivery via (surface or air) to 19000+ pin codes, catering to both individuals and businesses with a focus on speed and security

How do I use QuickPickup App?

Follow these simple steps to start leveraging Hassle-free goods transportation services from QuickPickup:

  1. Download the QuickPickup app
  2. Choose the service you want to use
  3. Select your pick-up and drop location
  4. Add any extra stops, if applicable
  5. Choose the most appropriate vehicle type as per your requirements (2wheeler, three-wheelers or trucks)
  6. Select the type of goods you’re sending
  7. Choose your payment method
  8. Click on “Book Now” and your vehicle is on its way!

Does QuickPickup transport between cities?

With QuickPickup Intercity Courier Service, you can choose to send courier and parcels across India to 19000+ pincodes via surface or air. All at economical rates.

What are the charges for goods transportation services by truck and two-wheelers ?

The charges for goods transportation services by truck and two-wheelers are based on the number of kilometres travelled and the type of vehicle chosen and this differs from locality to locality. You can also check the estimate tab on the QuickPickup website to get an exact idea of your final fare amount. Alternatively, the final fare amount can be determined when making the booking on the app.

Does QuickPickup provide Packers and Movers services?

Yes, QuickPickup caters to all kinds of house shifting needs through QuickPickup Packers & Movers. If you have an immediate moving requirement, we also have the option to hire a mini truck with labour. Intracity shifting services through QuickPickup Packers and Movers is available in Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Surat, Coimbatore, Indore, Jaipur, Lucknow and Nagpur. Intercity Packers and Movers services can be availed between any of the following cities - Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata and Ahmedabad. If you don’t see your city in the above list, don’t worry; the service will be launching soon in other cities. What types of goods does QuickPickup transport?

From a pin to an entire house, we will help transport anything, anywhere, anytime. However, QuickPickup does not help in the transportation of prohibited goods.

How long does it take to transport goods via QuickPickup?

When choosing on-demand goods transport services via trucks and two-wheelers, transportation takes place via road. The distance and traffic play a major role in determining the delivery time.

When choosing QuickPickup Intercity Courier Services via air or ground, there are multiple options to choose from. Some packages can be transported to other cities within a couple of days, or if you opt for express air intercity courier services, items can be delivered within a shorter timeline.

Does QuickPickup provide mini trucks like tata ace on monthly contract?

QuickPickup does not provide vehicles on contract or rental basis. We provide on-demand availability of mini trucks like Tata Ace. For bulk orders and frequent uses, we provide QuickPickup Enterprise service with one app-multi-city-mutli user access.

How does an API Integration with QuickPickup work?

QuickPickup's APIs are simple to integrate and use. The simple layouts will be easily adaptable and will start creating orders directly from your order management system.

How does an API integration solve business problems?

An API Integration with QuickPickup helps companies with webhook updates, live tracking, and delivery authentication across 16 cities. Utilise a standardised 2-wheeler service with zero hassles.

What vehicle categories are available via the APIs?

QuickPickup's APIs are currently available on Two-Wheelers. API integration on other vehicles will be launched soon.
